Cash McBride
Hello, my name is Cash McBride and I am a self proclaimed adventurer. By adventurer I really mean that my wife and I like to travel. We've been to many wonderful destinations.
The first trip we ever went on was to New Mexico and Colorado! I had watched a show about anciet astronauts that had supposedly visited the American Southwest and wanted to see it for myself. I was able to talk my wife (girlfriend at the time) to come along and it was amazing.
The second trip we went on was a little over a year later to Jamaica! Originally we had just planned a simple vacation but it ended up being a destination wedding. It rained the first two days pretty much non stop but it gave us plenty of time for the wedding planning. After that it was absolutely perfect.
We have been on a few trips since. Went back to Jamaica then to Portugal and Ireland. We had such a blast in Portugal including a dinner at a Michelin Star restaurant. In Ireland we visited the sites in the North where they filmed the Winterfell scenes for Game of Thrones.
